FAQ

What is it?

Got enthu to learn? We are here to help you form teams and guide you with some interesting projects.

Got an idea for an app? Develop it with our expert guidance.

'It has been over 4 months since I went home. I don't want to stay back.'

You don't need to! Work from your home. Work when you are bored. Collaborate using Hangouts, Git, and a variety of other tools. Form teams with friends both near and far. Build that dream app you’ve always wanted to.

What do I get out of this?

That is pretty much entirely in your hands. This is our efforts to find the talented guys among you who also have enthu. And if you prove to be among that elite group, you get

  • to work on paid website development offers
  • preference during WebOps Coordship selections
  • ideas and teammates for future hackathons
  • resume pointers

‘I have no fundaes :(. I couldn’t attend any of the sessions due to sem.’

If you are reading this, then you must be having high enthu! And that is sufficient to get involved in Wired-in Winter! We will help you pick up the fundaes along the way.
